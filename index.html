Our Heart

A community woven together by faith and friendship.

Since our founding, Gospel Outreach has been a beacon of hope in Nyeri. We believe that the church is not a building, but a family of believers dedicated to living out the teachings of Christ in every aspect of life. Whether you're a lifelong believer or just beginning your walk with Christ, this is a place to grow.
