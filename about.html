Since 1994

A Heart for
Nyeri and Beyond.

Our story began in the lush landscapes of Nyeri, driven by a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where every soul feels the warmth of divine grace and the strength of community.

What started as a small prayer gathering has blossomed into a vibrant spiritual home. We are rooted in the deep red soil of our heritage, yet our branches reach out to embrace the future with hope and modern compassion.

"Rooted in Love, Growing in Grace."

Led by Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Our leadership team is dedicated to serving the congregation and the Nyeri community with humility and wisdom.
